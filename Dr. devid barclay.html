Priority Triage

Dental Emergency?

Severe pain, a knocked-out tooth, or structural failure? We prioritize emergency cases to ensure you are seen by a clinician as soon as possible.

Knocked-Out Tooth

Keep the tooth in milk or saline. Do not touch the root. Contact us immediately for a 30-minute triage.

Abscess / Infection

Swelling of the gums or face requires immediate antibiotic assessment and root canal triage.

Broken Crown/Bridge

Structural failure of an architectural unit requires immediate repair to prevent nerve exposure.
